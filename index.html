How it works

1. Pick a language

Open the trainer on moyamova.online and choose your language and word set.

2. Train with cards

You see a word and recall the translation. The system repeats hard words more often and tracks your mistakes.

3. Progress builds automatically

The trainer remembers what you already know. Statistics and mistakes are available right inside the app.

FAQ

Do I need to install anything?

No. moyamova runs directly in your browser. On mobile you can add it to the home screen so it behaves like a regular app.

Does it work offline?

Yes. After the first load the main files are cached. The trainer can work without a constant internet connection.

Where is my progress stored?

Your progress lives in your browser on this device. If you clear browser data or switch devices, the trainer starts fresh.

Which languages are available?

The list of languages and decks evolves. You will always see the current options directly inside the trainer.
